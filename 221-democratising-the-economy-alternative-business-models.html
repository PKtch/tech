2.2.1. Democratising the Economy: Alternative Business Models

October 7, 2019 by DiEM25 Tech Collective

Long-term Measures

Democratising structures of economic ownership can counter the consolidation of power of today’s platform monopolies. Platform co-operatives are models of economic exchange which are owned and governed by workers, users and other stakeholders, and often have social and ethical objectives. The EU should support such economic approaches. For in-depth discussion, please see Chapter 4.2.
